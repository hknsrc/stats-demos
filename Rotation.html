Rotation Intuition for EFA

This demo shows how the same broad structure can look different under unrotated, orthogonal, and oblique solutions. The purpose is to make rotation easier to understand visually.

Unrotated

Initial axes are not yet aligned with the main item pattern. Loadings often look more mixed and less interpretable.

Orthogonal Rotation

Axes rotate to improve simple structure, but they remain perpendicular. Factors are treated as uncorrelated.

Oblique Rotation

Axes rotate more freely and do not need to stay at 90°. This is useful when factors are expected to correlate.
